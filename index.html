About Us

Hi! I’m Sushma Verma, a passionate home cook bringing warm, healthy, and tasty meals to students and professionals who miss the comfort of home-cooked food.

I started Annapurna Tiffin Services in 2022 from my own kitchen in Malviya Nagar, Delhi. What began with a few tiffins for neighbors has now grown into a trusted meal service for dozens of happy customers.

  • Fresh, quality ingredients
  • Hygienic cooking & eco-friendly packaging
  • On-time daily delivery

Delivery Areas: Malviya Nagar, Saket, Hauz Khas, Green Park
